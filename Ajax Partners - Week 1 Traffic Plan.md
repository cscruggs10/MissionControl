# Week 1: Traffic Generation Plan for Ajax Partners

### CEO Content (Corey)
- [ ] **LinkedIn post announcing Ajax Partners** - Personal story about why you're building this, what problem you're solving
- [ ] **"Here's what I learned building [previous venture]"** thread - 5-7 insights that lead to "this is why we built Ajax Partners"

### Quick-Win Content
- [ ] **1 case study/use case page** - Pick your strongest AI automation example, write it up with before/after metrics
- [ ] **"AI Use Cases" interactive page** - Based on that toggle Jonathan mentioned, let users explore specific automation scenarios
- [ ] **3 LinkedIn carousel posts** - "5 ways AI can [solve X problem]", "Common automation mistakes", "ROI calculator for AI implementation"

## Day 3-4: Distribution & Engagement

### LinkedIn Strategy
- [ ] **Comment on 10 relevant posts daily** - Add genuine value, not "great post!" spam
- [ ] **Engage with target audience** - Find CTOs/COOs/founders in your ICP, thoughtfully engage with their content
- [ ] **Share behind-the-scenes** - "Here's how we're building Ajax Partners" - people love transparency

### Community Outreach
- [ ] **Post in 3-5 relevant communities** (Reddit, Indie Hackers, relevant Slack/Discord groups) - Not spam, actual helpful content
- [ ] **Reach out to 10 warm connections** - Personal messages (not cold), "Hey, we just launched this, would love your feedback"

## Day 5-7: Amplification & Testing

### Partnerships & Mentions
- [ ] **Guest comment on industry newsletters** - Find 3-5 newsletters in your space, offer expert commentary
- [ ] **Reach out to complementary tools** - 5 non-competing tools your customers might use, explore content partnerships
- [ ] **Product Hunt soft launch prep** - If applicable, prepare for a future launch

### Paid Quick Tests (if budget allows)
- [ ] **$100 LinkedIn ad test** - Promote your best-performing organic post to target audience
- [ ] **Retargeting pixel setup** - Get this running now so you're capturing early visitors

### Analytics & Iteration
- [ ] **GA4 goals configured** - Track key actions (form fills, demo requests, specific page visits)
- [ ] **Daily traffic check-in** - Review what's working, double down on winners
- [ ] **Create traffic dashboard** - Simple daily snapshot: visitors, sources, conversions
