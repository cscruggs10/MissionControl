### 1. The Augusta Rule ($14K Tax-Free Loophole) ⭐ HIGHEST VIRAL POTENTIAL
**Hook Angle:** "The IRS loophole hiding in plain sight — rent your home to your business 14 days/year, pocket $14K+ tax-free. Here's how:"

**Target Pain Point:** Business owners leaving money on the table with unknown tax strategies

**Key Stats/Insights:**
- Section 280A allows 14-day rental exclusion
- Average rates: $1K-$2K/day = $14K-$28K tax-free annually
- Must be legitimate business purpose (meetings, retreats, conferences)
- No reporting requirement (not on Schedule E)
- Works for S-Corps, C-Corps, LLCs

**Thread Structure (8-10 tweets):**
1. Hook + shocking number
2. What is Augusta Rule (history)
3. How it works mechanically
4. Real-world example with numbers
5. IRS requirements (legitimacy)
6. Common mistakes to avoid
7. Who qualifies
8. CTA: Learn more strategies

**Engagement Triggers:**
- Dollar amounts (specific, tangible)
- "IRS loophole" language
- Simple, actionable
- Relatability (home = asset)

**Compliance Notes for Prowl:**
- Emphasize "legitimate business purpose" requirement
- Avoid "guaranteed" language
- Mention "consult tax advisor" disclaimer
- Focus on education, not promises
