# Product Marketing Context - Deal Machine Wholesale Vehicles

## Product Overview

**Deal Machine** is Corey's wholesale auto business. We acquire quality used vehicles and sell them to independent dealers, small lots, and flippers across the region.

**Business Model:**
- Acquire: Wholesale auctions, trade-ins, direct purchases
- Inspect: Palmer handles field inspections and video uploads
- List: Wheeljack creates Deal Machine listings
- Market: Axel (me) drives sales through targeted outreach
- Sell: Fast turnover (7-14 day avg), cash/wire transactions

**Platform:** dealerdealmachine.com

## Target Buyers (Dealer Personas)

### Persona 1: "The Regular" (40% of sales)
**Profile:**
- Independent dealer, 1-2 lot locations
- Buys 2-4 vehicles per month
- Budget: $10k-$25k per vehicle
- Quick decision maker (responds within hours)

**Vehicle Preferences:**
- Trucks (F-150, Silverado, RAM)
- SUVs (Tahoe, Yukon, Explorer, 4Runner)
- Condition: Clean title, under 150k miles
- Price sweet spot: $12k-$20k

**Communication:**
- Prefers SMS for quick hits
- Email for detailed specs/photos
- Responds to urgency ("couple guys asking about it")

**Examples:**
- Marcus: Loves Yukons, buys every 2 months, $15-20k range
- John: F-150 specialist, needs 4x4, budget flexible
- DeShawn: SUV lot, prefers low mileage, quick payer

### Persona 2: "The Flipper" (30% of sales)
**Profile:**
- Individual reseller, eBay/Craigslist/Facebook Marketplace
- Buys 1-2 vehicles per month
- Budget: $5k-$15k per vehicle
- Price-sensitive, needs margin

**Vehicle Preferences:**
- Popular models that move fast
- Lower price point ($8k-$12k)
- Minor cosmetic issues OK (they'll detail)
- Good mechanical condition critical

**Communication:**
- Email with detailed photos/video
- Wants CarFax, history, all details upfront
- Negotiates on price

**Examples:**
- Tony: Flips SUVs on Facebook, needs deals under $10k
- Lisa: eBay specialist, loves crossovers, picky on condition

### Persona 3: "The Volume Buyer" (20% of sales)
**Profile:**
- Mid-size dealer group (3-5 locations)
- Buys 5-10 vehicles per month
- Budget: $15k-$35k per vehicle
- Relationship-driven, loyal if treated right

**Vehicle Preferences:**
- Diverse inventory (trucks, SUVs, sedans)
- Higher quality, lower mileage preferred
- Will pay premium for clean units
- Wants first look at new arrivals

**Communication:**
- Weekly inventory emails
- Direct line to Corey for big purchases
- Appreciates curated lists ("here's 5 for you")

**Examples:**
- Big Mike's Auto Group: Buys anything clean, pays fast
- Rodriguez Motors: Prefers trucks, needs volume

### Persona 4: "The Specialist" (10% of sales)
**Profile:**
- Niche dealer (trucks only, luxury only, etc.)
- Buys 1-3 vehicles per month
- Budget: varies widely
- Very specific requirements

**Vehicle Preferences:**
- Specific makes/models only
- Will wait for the right unit
- Price less important than specs

**Communication:**
- "Let me know when you get [specific vehicle]"
- Low volume but high value
- Long-term relationship

**Examples:**
- Truck Haven: F-150/Silverado only, 4x4 required
- Luxury Motors: German cars, low mileage only

## Vehicle Categories & Performance

### Top Sellers (Fast Movers)
1. **Full-Size Trucks** (F-150, Silverado, RAM)
   - Avg days on market: 5
   - Price range: $15k-$25k
   - Buyer type: Regulars, Volume Buyers

2. **Full-Size SUVs** (Tahoe, Yukon, Expedition)
   - Avg days on market: 7
   - Price range: $18k-$30k
   - Buyer type: Regulars, Flippers

3. **Mid-Size SUVs** (Explorer, 4Runner, Grand Cherokee)
   - Avg days on market: 10
   - Price range: $12k-$22k
   - Buyer type: All personas

### Moderate Sellers
4. **Crossovers** (RAV4, CR-V, Equinox)
   - Avg days on market: 12
   - Price range: $10k-$18k
   - Buyer type: Flippers, Volume Buyers

5. **Mid-Size Trucks** (Tacoma, Colorado, Ranger)
   - Avg days on market: 14
   - Price range: $14k-$24k
   - Buyer type: Specialists

### Slow Movers (Strategic Inventory)
6. **Sedans** (Accord, Camry, Altima)
   - Avg days on market: 18
   - Price range: $8k-$15k
   - Buyer type: Flippers, Budget buyers

## Pricing Strategy

**Sweet Spots:**
- Under $12k: Flipper territory, fast turnover
- $12k-$20k: Regular dealer bread & butter
- $20k-$30k: Volume buyers, quality units
- Over $30k: Specialist inventory, slower move

**Competitive Advantage:**
- 10-15% below retail market
- Clean CarFax, detailed inspections
- Professional video walkarounds (Palmer)
- Fast transactions, no games

## Geographic Market

## Messaging Framework

### Value Propositions

**For Regulars:**
> "First look at quality inventory before it hits the market"

**For Flippers:**
> "Profit margin built in - clean units at wholesale pricing"

**For Volume Buyers:**
> "Reliable supply, consistent quality, no surprises"

**For Specialists:**
> "We'll find what you need - built our network on it"

### Objection Handling

**"Price too high"**
→ "This is 15% below retail - check comparable units yourself"

**"Can you do better?"**
→ "Price is firm, but I can show you other units in your range"

**"Need more photos/info"**
→ "Full video walkthrough + CarFax in the listing - what else do you need?"

**"I'll think about it"**
→ "No problem - heads up, had 2 other inquiries today. Want me to ping you if it's still here tomorrow?"
