# Palmer's Guide to Using Derek

**Derek** is your wholesale sales agent. He has access to all 22 dealer contacts in Go High Level and can send text/email blasts instantly.

## How to Use Derek

### Send Messages to Specific Dealers

**Via Telegram (through Iris):**
```
"Derek, send this to sebastian: Got a clean Yukon for you, $15,775. Want first look?"
```

**To multiple dealers:**
```
"Derek, send this to sebastian and sam: [your message]"
```

### Send to Dealer Groups (Tags)

**All dealers with specific tag:**
```
"Derek, blast this to all independent sub prime dealers: [your message]"
```

**Available tags in GHL:**
- `independent sub prime`
- `bhph`
- `new car jd clean`

### When You Create a New Listing

**Derek will ask you:**
> "New listing: 2018 F-150 XLT, 95k miles, $22,500. Who should I send this to?"

**You reply:**
- "Send to sebastian and sam" (specific dealers)
- "All independent sub prime" (by tag)
- "Hold for now" (don't send yet)

Derek will send immediately based on your instruction.

## What Derek Can Do

✅ Send SMS to any dealer instantly  
✅ Send email blasts  
✅ Target by dealer name or tags  
✅ Follow up on your behalf  
✅ Track who was contacted in GHL

## What Derek Can't Do

❌ Negotiate prices (that's Corey's call)  
❌ Change listing details (that's your job)  
❌ Send without your instruction (for now - always asks first)

## Commands You Can Use

```
"Derek, send [message] to [dealer names]"
"Derek, blast this to all [tag]"
"Derek, follow up with [dealer] about [vehicle]"
"Derek, who bought F-150s in the last 3 months?"
```

## Response Time

Derek checks every 15 minutes (or instantly if we set up webhooks). He'll confirm when messages are sent:

> ✅ Sent to 3 dealers: sebastian, sam, marcus

**Need help?** Ask Iris or Corey. Derek is here to make your job easier - use him as much as you need!
