## 1. Market Context

### The Captive Insurance Opportunity

**What is captive insurance?**
Captive insurance allows businesses to create their own insurance companies to self-insure risks. When structured properly with legitimate domiciles and regulatory compliance, it provides:
- Tax-advantaged premium deductions
- Asset protection benefits
- Risk management control
- Potential investment growth

**Market dynamics:**
- Established, legal strategy (not a "loophole")
- Requires sophisticated setup and management
- Most business owners don't know it exists
- Most CPAs/advisors don't offer it (infrastructure barrier)

**Ajax Partners' positioning:**
We remove the infrastructure barrier for advisors while creating deal flow through direct business owner capture.

---

### The Two-Market Challenge

**Problem 1: Business Owners Don't Know About It**
- Active search demand for "how to reduce business taxes"
- Captive insurance rarely appears in results
- Education gap = opportunity

**Problem 2: Advisors Can't Offer It (Commoditization)**
- Traditional CPAs/wealth advisors compete on price
- Clients see them as interchangeable
- Need differentiation but lack unique strategies
- Building captive infrastructure = too expensive/complex

**Our Solution: The Flywheel**
```
Business owner searches tax savings
→ Finds Alpha Directory (our SEO)
→ Submits info to connect with strategic advisor
→ We capture lead + route to partner advisor
→ Advisor introduces captive strategy
→ Advisor looks like genius (differentiated)
→ Advisor wants more deal flow
→ Advisor refers captive services to other clients
→ More business owners hear about it
→ Repeat
```

### Track 2: Wealth Systems Channels

#### Loom Videos (Primary Outreach Tool)

**Strategy:** Personalized video messages to target CPAs/advisors

**Why Loom:**
- Shows face = builds trust before meeting
- 2-3 min to record, feels like 10x effort to recipient
- Higher response rate than cold email alone
- Demonstrates you did homework on them

**Loom Script Framework:**
1. Personal opener (something specific about them)
2. Pattern interrupt: "You're not commoditized — but your clients might think you are"
3. Value prop: "I help advisors like you offer tax-advantaged strategies to business owner clients"
4. Differentiation hook: "Without building infrastructure yourself"
5. Soft CTA: "Worth a 15-min call to see if there's a fit?"

**Volume Target:**
- Week 1-2: 10-20 Looms (learning/testing)
- Week 3-4: 20-30 Looms (scaling)
- Month 2+: 50-100 Looms/month (systematic outreach)

**Success Metrics:**
- View rate (target: 60%+)
- Reply rate (target: 10-15%)
- Meeting booking rate (target: 5-8%)

---

#### Cold Calling

**Strategy:** Direct conversations with warm-ish prospects

**When to call:**
- After Loom sent (day 3-5 follow-up)
- Loom viewed but no reply
- High-value targets (large RIAs, established CPAs)

**Call Script Framework:**
1. Pattern interrupt opener (not "How are you today?")
2. Permission-based: "Did I catch you at a bad time?"
3. Value hook (10 seconds): "I work with CPAs who want to offer tax-advantaged strategies without building infrastructure"
4. Goal: Book 15-min discovery call (not sell on first call)

**Volume Target:**
- Week 1-2: 20-30 dials (testing script)
- Week 3-4: 30-50 dials (refining)
- Month 2+: 100+ dials/month

**Success Metrics:**
- Connect rate (target: 20-30%)
- Meeting booking rate (target: 10-15% of connects)

---

#### D8: Discovery Call Framework

**Goal:** Qualify advisor fit and book next step (partnership agreement or demo)

**Agenda (15 minutes):**
1. Intro (2 min) — Who you are, why you're calling
2. Qualify (5 min) — Ask questions about their practice
3. Pitch (5 min) — Explain partnership value
4. Close (3 min) — Next steps or disqualify

---

**Qualifying Questions:**

1. "Tell me about your practice — how many business owner clients do you serve?"
2. "What's your typical client revenue range?"
3. "Do you currently offer any tax-advantaged strategies beyond standard deductions?"
4. "Have you heard of captive insurance before? What's your familiarity level?"
5. "What would make a partnership like this valuable for you — is it differentiation? Deal flow? Revenue share?"

---

**Partnership Pitch (If Qualified):**

"Great — here's how it works:

**What we do:**
- We handle all captive insurance setup and backend (domicile, compliance, management)
- We send you qualified business owner leads through our Alpha Directory
- We provide education and support (you don't need to be a captive insurance expert)

**What you do:**
- Introduce the strategy to your clients when appropriate
- Maintain client relationship
- Refer clients to us for captive setup

**What you get:**
- Differentiation (offer something competitors can't)
- Deal flow (qualified business owner leads from our directory)
- Revenue share ([% TBD] of captive management fees)
- Ongoing support and education

**What happens next:**
- I send you partnership agreement (simple, no exclusivity)
- You review, sign if it makes sense
- We onboard you (add you to Alpha Directory, give you marketing materials)
- We start sending leads your way
- You introduce first client to captive strategy

Sound good?"

---

**Disqualification Criteria:**
- Serves too few business owner clients (<10)
- Client revenue too low (<$1M average)
- Not interested in partnership model (wants to build in-house)
- Compliance concerns they're not willing to work through

- **If interested:** Send partnership agreement via email, schedule follow-up call in 3-5 days

- **If not interested:** Thank them, ask if they know anyone else who might be interested
