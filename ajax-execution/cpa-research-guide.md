# CPA Research Guide - Finding Real Targets for Each Market

*Use this guide to populate the 25-CPA target list without needing account access.*

---

## Method 1: Google Search (Free, No Account Needed)

**Search pattern for each market:**

```
"CPA firm" site:linkedin.com [city], [state]
"tax advisor" "business owner" [city], [state]
"captive insurance advisor" [city], [state]
"accounting firm" "8-15 employees" [city], [state]
```

**Process for each market:**
1. Paste search query into Google (with city/state filled in)
2. Find LinkedIn company pages + individual CPA profiles
3. Check firmsize (click company page → "Company size: X employees")
4. Extract: Name, title, firm, location
5. Go to firm website → find email format (usually firstname@firmname.com)
6. Verify email with quick test (LinkedIn message)

**Time per market:** 20-30 minutes for 5 good CPAs

---

## Method 2: LinkedIn Search (Requires Account, But Free)

**LinkedIn search string:**

```
(title:CPA OR title:Accountant OR title:Advisor) AND (location:[city])
AND (current company size: 6-50 employees)
AND (recent activity in last 30 days)
```

**Process:**
1. Search
2. Scroll through results
3. Check individual profiles:
   - Does their headline show business owner focus? (tax, strategy, advisory)
   - Do they have recent posts/activity? (last month)
   - Is their firm mid-size (not Big 4, not solo)
4. Click to firm profile → get firm website
5. Go to firm website → contact page → extract email

**Time per market:** 15-20 minutes for 5 good CPAs

```
- [ ] Business owner focus (visible in posts/about)

- [ ] Mid-size firm (8-15 people ideal)

```

## Email Verification (No Account Needed)

For each email found, verify it's real:

**Method 1: Website Contact Form**
- Go to firm website
- Check "Contact Us" page
- Verify the name/email format
- Send test message: "Hi [name], wanted to verify I have the right email?"

**Method 2: LinkedIn Message**
- Go to their LinkedIn profile
- Send message: "Hi [name], what's the best email to reach you?"
- Wait for response (usually within 24h)

**Method 3: Phone Call**
- Call the firm: "Hi, I'm trying to reach [name]. Can you verify the best email?"
- They usually just tell you

**Mark each email:**
- ✅ High confidence (verified via LinkedIn or website)
- ❓ Medium confidence (likely format, not verified)
- ❌ Low confidence (guess, skip)
