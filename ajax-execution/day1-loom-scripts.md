# Loom Video Scripts for CPA/Advisor Outreach

*Created: Feb 12, 2026 — 12:10 PM UTC*
*Framework: 60-90 second personalized video patterns*

---

## The Purpose of Loom

**Why it works:**
- 60-90 seconds of personalized attention = trust builder
- Shows your face + personality (can't be ignored like email)
- Takes 2-3 min to record, feels like 10x effort to recipient
- Reply rate 2-3x higher than cold email alone
- Natural transition to phone call

**How to film:**
- Light yourself well (window light, no backlight)
- Slightly off-center (more natural than dead-center)
- Script is your guide, not a teleprompter read
- Genuine smile, natural pace (slightly slower than normal speech)
- Show your desk/background (humanizes you)
- Reference their website/profile in background if possible (extra personalization)

## Template 1: TAX-FOCUSED CPA OPENER

**Target:** CPAs specializing in business owner taxes/tax planning

**Hook Opening:**
"Hi [Name], I came across your [website / LinkedIn article / firm page] and noticed you do great work helping business owners with [specific thing they mentioned — e.g., 'S-corp strategy' or 'tax savings']. I wanted to reach out personally because I think you're missing one strategy that most CPAs don't know about."

**Value Layer (30-40 seconds):**
"I work with forward-thinking CPAs who want to add a genuinely unique strategy to their toolkit without building the infrastructure themselves. It's around captive insurance — specifically, how to help your business owner clients create legitimate tax deductions of $100K-$500K+ while building tax-deferred wealth inside their company."

"It's not a loophole. It's a strategy that's been around 30+ years. We just handle the technical infrastructure, so you can focus on the client relationship and look like a genius."

**Soft CTA (10-15 seconds):**
"I think this could be really valuable for your practice, especially if your business owner clients are always asking 'how can I reduce my taxes?' — which I'm guessing they are. Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call this week to see if it's a fit?"

**Closer:**
"Let me know — [link or email]. Thanks, [Name]."

**Total length:** 60-75 seconds

**Script checklist:**
- [ ] Reference something specific about them
- [ ] Clear value prop (what it does, who it serves)
- [ ] Why they should care (differentiates them, serves their clients)
- [ ] Soft CTA (not demanding, permission-based)
- [ ] Your name + how to reach you

## General Loom Guidelines

### Filming Tips
- **Lighting:** Natural window light on your face (not shadowed)
- **Background:** Your real desk/office (shows you're a real person, not a corporation)
- **Framing:** Slightly off-center, head-and-shoulders shot
- **Pace:** 10-15% slower than normal conversation
- **Smile:** Natural, not forced
- **Eyes:** Look at the camera slightly above the lens (more personal)
- **No scripted reads:** Script guides you, you speak naturally
- **One take if possible:** Slight imperfections = authenticity

### Script Rules
- Reference something specific about them (not just their industry)
- Value prop in one sentence (if they have to think about it, you lost them)
- Why they should care personally (not generic)
- Soft CTA (not "let's get on a call" immediately)
- Your name + how to reach you clearly stated

### Subject Line for Loom Send
- "Quick video from [Your name]"
- "Thought of you — 90 second video"
- "[Name], quick message for you"
- Nothing salesy in subject (defeats the purpose)

### Include with Loom
- Subject line should make them want to click
- Body text: 2-3 sentences context + link
- Calendar link or phone # (reduce friction to say yes)
- Keep email body short (you did the talking in the video)

**Example email with Loom:**
```
Hi [Name],

I came across your firm + thought you'd be worth a quick conversation. Recorded a 90-second video — would appreciate if you could give it a minute.

[Loom link]

Open to a 15-min call this week?

[Calendar link or phone #]

[Your name]
```

### Metrics to Track
- **View rate:** What % actually watch (vs. just get it)?
- **Completion rate:** Do they watch to the end?
- **Reply rate:** Do they respond (not just watch)?
- **Booking rate:** What % of views → meetings booked?

## Production Workflow

**Per week:**
- Record 10-15 personalized Looms (takes 30-45 min total)
- Batch record (don't do one at a time)
- Use same outfit/lighting/background (consistency)
- Save each with clear name: `[FirstName]-[Company]-[Date]`
- Upload to Loom, share link
- Customize email + send

**Tools:**
- Loom (free or Pro depending on analytics needs)
- Calendar software (Calendly/Cal.com for booking link)
- CRM to track views + engagement

## Notes

- Loom videos outperform cold email significantly (2-3x reply rate)
- Best time to send: Tues-Thurs, 10-11am their timezone
- Follow-up with email 3 days later if no reply

- Track what gets replies most (refine approach)
